# Testing

To test your Mini App you can use the developer mode inside of World App. All Mini Apps must be created in the <Link href="https://developer.worldcoin.org/">Developer Portal</Link> first in order to test.

### Enable Developer Mode and Load your Mini App
1. Go in to the settings of World App and click the version number 7 times.
2. Accept the modal that appears, you are now in developer mode.
3. Click on Developer Options and enter your `app_id` and `api_key` from the <Link href="https://developer.worldcoin.org/">Developer Portal</Link>.

You should now see your mini app loaded in World App.
